Logos

Open Tea News

Open Tea News is a news website seeking to advance web technology and web glamor. The logo is inspired by the art deco movement of the early 1900's. The logo uses a simplified, minimalistic interpretation of the art deco style.

Visit OTN's website

Oats and Apples

Oats and Apples is a conceptual brand created as a branding research project. The logo was designed to represent a sun rising over the apex of a mountain.

Wave Digital Ministries

This logo was formed to replace the ministry's less recognizable "Blue Wave" logo. The logo was formed to incorporate the ministry's faith and initials. The "W" forms an EKG wave and is encapsulated by a larger "C" to represent Christ.

See WDM's website
